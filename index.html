Our Story

Elevate (ELEV8) Music, a dynamic Afrobeats cover band, harmoniously formed in the summer of 2022 with a shared mission to elevate audiences through their soulful melodies. Our repertoire extends across a vibrant spectrum of African and other popular music. With a commitment to musical excellence, we are a rhythmic force that seeks to uplift and inspire through electrifying performances.
